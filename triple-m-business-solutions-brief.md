# Triple M Business Solutions — Website Project Brief

## Business Name
Triple M Business Solutions (DBA)
Named after the initials of the founder's two daughters — "MMM." Family-driven brand story.

## What the Business Does
A business consulting firm focused on bookkeeping, QuickBooks Online (QBO) organization, and back-office infrastructure build-out for small businesses — with a specialty in construction and trade companies, though not limited to that market.

## Primary Target Market
Small construction and trade companies that need to build out operational infrastructure (accounting, HR, compliance, insurance, bonding) but don't have the resources for a full internal back office.

## Founder Credentials
- QuickBooks Online (QBO) Certified
- OSHA 30 Certified
- Minor in Business, Texas A&M (coursework completed)
- 5+ years managing full back-office operations within the construction industry
- 10+ years prior business consulting experience implementing payroll, HR, HRIS, Workers Comp, and health insurance programs for small-to-mid-size businesses
- Not a CPA or Tax Accountant — works as a liaison to CPAs and tax professionals

## Services Offered
- Bookkeeping and QBO setup/organization
- Health insurance negotiation
- General Liability insurance negotiation and auditing
- Workers Comp audits
- Document management (W-9s, I-9s, 1099 issuance)
- 401(k) implementation
- Surety/bonding coordination
- Contract development
- Time tracking and PTO system implementation
- Credit card transaction reconciliation systems
- Cash flow management
- Line of credit acquisition support (financial reporting/packaging)
- CPA liaison for tax reporting
- Project profitability projections
- Procore billing support
- Safety program development

## Brand Positioning
Family-driven, hands-on, and outcomes-focused — not a distant advisory service. Positioned as an embedded operations partner who builds and runs the back office, not just an outside consultant who advises from a distance.

## Service/Pricing Model
1. **Diagnostic / Onboarding Assessment** — flat-fee initial engagement to evaluate the client's current systems and compliance exposure before any ongoing commitment.
2. **One-off Projects** — set flat rate for defined-scope work (e.g., QBO cleanup, contract drafting, safety program build).
3. **Hourly Rate** — for minor, ad hoc background work.
4. **Monthly Retainer (tiered)** — full-access ongoing service, reviewed every 3 months. Suggested tiers:
   - *Essentials*: bookkeeping, QBO management, reporting
   - *Growth*: adds HR systems, time tracking, cash flow management
   - *Full Infrastructure*: adds insurance negotiation, bonding/surety coordination, tax liaison, 401(k) implementation
5. **Seasonal / Compliance Packages** — standalone flat-fee packages for annual/periodic needs (Workers Comp audit prep, GL audit prep, 1099 season, bonding renewals).
